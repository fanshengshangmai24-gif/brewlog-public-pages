# BrewLog Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-19

BrewLog is designed as a local-first coffee brewing log app.

## Data We Store

The app stores brew logs, beans, recipes, settings, and reminder preferences on your device using Apple-provided local storage.

## Data Collection

BrewLog does not operate an external server and does not sell personal data. The app does not intentionally collect analytics data, tracking identifiers, location data, contacts, photos, or health data.

## Purchases

If you purchase BrewLog Pro, purchase processing is handled by Apple through the App Store. BrewLog checks App Store entitlements to unlock paid features.

## Notifications

If you enable reminders, BrewLog schedules local notifications on your device. Notification settings can be changed in the app or in iOS Settings.

## Data Export and Deletion

You can export brew logs as CSV from the app where available. You can delete local app data from the Settings screen in BrewLog.
